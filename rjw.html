RUN-JOG-WALK-A-THON

What is the RJW?

We as a non-profit ministry do not involve ourselves in many fundraiser's, but our Run-Jog-Walk-A-Thon has been around for the past thirty three years. The Run-Jog-Walk-A-Thon has provided an opportunity for thousands of campers to attend camp and hear the gospel of Jesus Christ. The money goes to the Marnie Provance Memorial Scholarship Fund. Marnie died in a drowning accident in 1981 while attending our camp. We put the Run-Jog-Walk-A-Thon money in this memorial scholarship and give it directly to churches that have students that cannot afford to come to camp. Your financial participation in this fund raiser is an amazing way to spread the good news of a loving God to teenagers from Washington, Colorado, Idaho, Texas, Oregon, and California.

How it Works

Our staff will run-jog-walk around a 1/4- mile track for one hour on the Sunday of the Memorial Day Retreat. Our staff secure pledges from friends and relatives per lap that they complete, or a flat donation here online. This year each staff members goal is to raise a minimum of $600.00.

The goal

Last year our staff raised $60,034.91. These funds went out to churches in the form of camper scholarships. With the current economy it is difficult for families to send their kids to camp. That is where you come in! Your donation towards the R-J-W directly sends kids to camp. We ask that you help us in making this a success.

How to Give

Find the staff member you would like to donate towards in the drop down menu below, select their name, then click donate. You will be directed to PAYPAL where you can make a donation. This will go directly into the Marnie Provance Memorial Scholarship Fund in the name of the staff you selected. If you want to make a pledge per lap, click here.
